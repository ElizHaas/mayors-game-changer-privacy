Privacy Policy – Mayors Game Changer

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Mayors Game Changer is a city-facing planning and exploration tool designed to help mayors and economic development teams explore emerging sports and evaluate potential city-fit considerations. The tool is informational and advisory in nature.

Information We Collect

The tool may process information voluntarily provided by users during interactions, including but not limited to:

  • City names and high-level city characteristics
  • Planning priorities or goals shared by the user
  • References to public facilities, venues, or publicly available information

The tool does not require users to provide personal identifying information about individuals.

How Information Is Used

Information provided during use of this tool is used solely to:

  • Generate planning-oriented responses
  • Explore illustrative city–sport alignment considerations
  • Improve relevance and clarity of responses within the session

The tool does not use information to make decisions, rankings, or guarantees.

Data Storage and Retention

This tool does not independently store, retain, or manage user data outside of the platform on which it is hosted. Data handling and retention are governed by the policies of the hosting platform.

Data Sharing

Information provided to the tool is not sold or shared with third parties by the tool itself. Any data processing is subject to the policies of the underlying platform and service provider.

Use by Public Officials

This tool is intended for exploratory planning use and does not replace professional, legal, financial, or policy advice.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in functionality or governance. Updates will be posted to this page.
